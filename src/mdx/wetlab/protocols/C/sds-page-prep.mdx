###### Materials
- 4× SDS buffer
- Samples (supernatant, pellet, total lysate)
- Milli-Q water
- DTT

###### Prepare (examples)
- **Induced supernatant:** 3.333 µL 4× buffer + 1.27 µL sample + 8.73 µL H₂O + 3.333 µL DTT  
- **Uninduced supernatant:** 3.333 µL buffer + 1.6 µL sample + 8.4 µL H₂O + 3.333 µL DTT  
- **Induced pellet:** 3.333 µL buffer + 10 µL sample + 3.333 µL DTT  
- **Uninduced pellet:** 3.333 µL buffer + 1.2 µL sample + 8.8 µL H₂O + 3.333 µL DTT  
- **Total lysate (induced):** 3.333 µL buffer + 10 µL sample + 3.333 µL DTT  
- **Total lysate (uninduced):** 3.333 µL buffer + 10 µL sample + 3.333 µL DTT  

**Heat all samples at 95 °C for 5 min.**

###### Materials
- Precast SDS-PAGE gel (e.g., Bio-Rad 4568021)
- Electrophoresis cell
- 1× Tris-Glycine-SDS running buffer
- Protein ladder

###### Methods
1. Mount gel; remove bottom tape; assemble in gel box.  
   Fill inner chamber to top and outer to "2 gels" line with running buffer.
2. Load **5 µL** ladder and samples (record lane map).
3. Run **180 V for 30 min**.

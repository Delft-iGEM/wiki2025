###### Materials
- Kanamycin sulfate powder
- Sterile Milli-Q water
- 0.22 µm syringe filter
- Sterile 50 mL tube

###### Methods
1. Dissolve **250 mg** kanamycin sulfate to **50 mL** (→ **50 mg/mL**).
2. Filter-sterilize (**0.22 µm**) into a sterile tube.
3. Aliquot (e.g., **3 mL**), label; **store −20 °C**.

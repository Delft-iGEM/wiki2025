###### Materials
- Clean glassware
- DI water
- 50 mL tubes / bottles
- 0.5 M KH₂PO₄ (acid stock)
- 0.5 M K₂HPO₄ (base stock)
- pH meter (calibrated at 4/7/10)
- 37 °C water bath

###### Methods
1. Combine phosphate stocks (per 30 mL, 0.05 M buffer):  
   - pH 6.0 → 0.175 mL K₂HPO₄ + 2.825 mL KH₂PO₄  
   - pH 6.8 → 0.84 mL K₂HPO₄ + 2.16 mL KH₂PO₄  
   - pH 7.4 → 1.825 mL K₂HPO₄ + 1.175 mL KH₂PO₄  
2. Add ~25 mL DI water, mix gently.  
3. Check and adjust pH as needed.  
4. Bring to 30 mL total volume.  
5. Warm to 37 °C before use.

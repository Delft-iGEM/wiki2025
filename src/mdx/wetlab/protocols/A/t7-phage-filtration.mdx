
###### Materials

* 0.2 μm filters
* 20 mL syringes with spiral attachment for filters
* Centrifuge (Capable of 3,000 rpm)
* 50 mL tubes
* Overnight T7 phage reboot sample

###### Procedure

1. Centrifuge overnight T7 phage reboot sample at 3,000 rpm for 12 min
2. In a lab area intended for phage work, transfer supernatant into a 20 mL syringe with a filter attached at its end.
3. Push the sample through the filter into a new 50 mL flask.
4. Store T7 phage sample at 4 °C

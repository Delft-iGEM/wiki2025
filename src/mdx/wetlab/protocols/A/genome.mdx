**Materials**

-   NEBuilder® HiFi DNA Assembly Kit
-   DNA fragments (wild-type and mCherry insertion fragments)
-   NEBuilder Calculator
-   Thermocycler (capable of 50 °C)

**Procedure**

0.	Aliquot 10 μL Assembly Mix in 0.2 mL tubes. 
1.	Pipette carefully in corresponding volumes of MilliQ water and T7 genome DNA fragments into the tube with the assembly mix. Mix carefully by pipetting up and down. 
2.	Incubate at 50 °C for 1 h. 
3.	Store the final product at -20 °C or directly use it for transformation.

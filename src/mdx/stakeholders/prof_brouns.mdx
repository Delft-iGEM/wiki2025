Prof. Brouns discussed the options for bacterial hosts and phages with us. He highlighted the importance of tailoring our host to the gut, and working with well-established display systems. He also connected us with his colleague, Rita Martins Costa, who became an ongoing technical mentor for our experimental work. This first stakeholder meeting ensured that our design ideas could be translated into feasible plans.
